Consent: By using the app users agree that they consent to: 1. The conditions set out in this privacy policy; and 2. The collection, use, and retention of the data listed in this privacy policy.

Personal Data That is Collected: WeatherSpotter only collects data that is necessary to achieve the purposes that are outlines in the privacy policy. No additional data will be collected. Data Collected in a Non-Automatic Way: WeatherSpotter may collect the following when you perform certain functions on the app - Pictures and Videos - Location - Phone Number The data may be collected using the following methods - Pictures and Videos can be submitted by users through a form (file upload for reports and forum posts) - Location may be collected through 2 methods 1. Location may be collected using a geolocation function if users decide to allow their location to be used for a WeatherSpotter report; 2. Users that subscribe for SMS alerts will be asked to submit their approximate location through a registration form. - Phone numbers are only collected from those that choose to subscribe for SMS alerts. Subscribers will submit their phone number through a registration form.

How Personal Data is Used: Data that is collected on the app will only be used for the purposes that are outlined in this Privacy Policy or indicated on relevant pages on the app. Your data will not be used for purposes beyond what is disclosed in this Privacy Policy. The data that is collected when the user performs certain functions may be used for the following purposes: 1. Communication, sending out SMS messages to the cell phone of a subscribed user that has a significant weather event reported within 200 km of their reference address. 2. Visual aides (Photos or Videos) that are attached to WeatherSpotter reports for the purpose of conveying information. Users may be able to download photos and videos that are published to WeatherSpotter. 3. Location information is used to obtain accurate map coordinates for WeatherSpotter reports. SMS subscribers will need to provide their approximate location for the purpose of determining if a subscriber is within 200 km of the location of a new WeatherSpotter report. Third Parties: Users may need to share data with the following third parties; 1. Payment Processing Websites - Users that choose to subscribe for SMS alerts will need to enter their payment information into the payment processing website.

Cookie Policy: A cookie is a small file, stored on a user's hard drive by a website. Its purpose is to collect data relating to the user's browsing habits. You can choose to be notified each time a cookie is transmitted. You can also choose to disable cookies entirely in your internet browser. WeatherSpotter uses the following types of cookies, 1. Functional Cookies - Functional cookies are used to remember the selections made on the app so that your selections are saved for your next visits; and 2. Analytical Cookies - Analytical cookies collect data on how you access the app, for example data on the content you access and how long you stay on the app, etc.
